Why Your Tankless Water Heater Needs Flushing

Orange County's hard water is one of the toughest in the nation. Here's what's happening inside your system right now.

The Problem

Orange County's hard water contains high levels of minerals and sediment. Over time, these minerals accumulate inside your tankless water heater's heat exchanger, creating a thick mineral buildup (called scaling).

This causes:

  • Reduced hot water flow & pressure
  • Lower heating efficiency (higher bills)
  • Strange noises from your heater
  • Eventually: complete system failure

The Solution

Annual tankless water heater flushing removes mineral buildup and descales your system, restoring efficiency and preventing costly repairs.

  • Restores hot water flow & pressure
  • Improves energy efficiency (lower bills)
  • Eliminates strange noises
  • Extends system lifespan by years
  • Prevents emergency breakdowns
