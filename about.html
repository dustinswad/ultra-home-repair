About Ultra Home Repair

Your trusted partner for quality home repair services

Meet Dustin

Hi, my name is Dustin and I'm the owner of Ultra Home Repair, based right here in the Woodbury neighborhood of Irvine. I've spent over 15 years flipping houses, personally managing dozens of remodels ranging from small cosmetic upgrades to full-scale home renovations. I've also worked at a $60 million-a-year home renovation firm, giving me deep insight into how top-tier projects are managed.

I started this business because I saw a gap in the market: there are jobs that are too small for general contractors but too important to ignore. Most contractors won't even return your call for a sub-$1,000 job, or if they do, they'll charge more than it's worth. I keep things lean and local, which means I can deliver professional-quality work at a fraction of the cost.

I genuinely love this work. I love fixing things, building things, and most of all, I love the moment a homeowner breathes a sigh of relief when a nagging problem is finally handled.

Whether you need help with drywall repair, fixture installs, plumbing under the sink, or just crossing things off your home repair list—I'd be honored to be your go-to handyman.
