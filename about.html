About Ultra Home Repair

Your trusted partner for quality home repair services

Meet Dustin

I'm Dustin, owner of Ultra Home Repair and a long-time resident in the Woodbury neighborhood of Irvine. Since 2009, I've been fixing, building, and improving homes — from quick cosmetic touch-ups to larger repair projects. I've flipped houses, personally managed dozens of remodels, and even worked for a $60-million-a-year renovation firm, so I understand what it takes to deliver professional-quality results.

Why I Started Ultra Home Repair

There's a gap in the market. Many repairs are too small for general contractors but too important to ignore — especially when a closing date or new tenant is on the line. Most contractors won't return your call for a sub-$1,000 job, or they'll quote far more than it's worth.

Ultra Home Repair bridges that gap. We keep things lean and local, which means you get high-quality work without the high-end markup.

What You Can Expect

  • • Fast response times — because delays cost money.
  • • Clear, fair pricing — no surprises when the bill comes.
  • • Professional results — without the contractor overhead.

Whether you're a homeowner tackling a repair list, a Realtor with inspection fixes, or a property manager needing a reliable go-to, I make the process simple and stress-free.
